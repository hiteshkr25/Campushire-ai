How Hiring Drives Work

1
TPO Creates Drive The college TPO posts the drive with job role, salary range, CGPA cutoff, required skills, eligible branches, and interview rounds.
2
Students Apply Eligible students see the published drive and submit applications with their resume. The AI ATS scorer ranks them automatically.
3
You Evaluate & Interview Review candidate profiles, check ATS rankings, shortlist candidates, and schedule interview rounds (aptitude, technical, HR, etc.).
4
Release Offers For selected candidates, release a formal offer letter with the salary package, job location, joining date, and acceptance deadline.
